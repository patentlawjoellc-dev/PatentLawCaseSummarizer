- Appeal: 25-1514 / 25-1566
- Origin: PTO
- Document: Order (Nonprecedential)
- Disposition: Dismissed; mandate issued May 1, 2026.
- Tags: non-precedential, remand, standing / jurisdiction, Hatch-Waxman / ANDA

**Holding:** The Federal Circuit dismissed both the appeal and cross-appeal by stipulation of the parties under Fed. R. App. P. 42(b), with each side bearing its own costs.

**Why it matters:** Signals a settlement or other resolution between Meta and Eight KHz following IPR proceedings, leaving the PTAB's underlying decision undisturbed and unreviewed.

**Key points:**
- Both the appeal (25-1514) and cross-appeal (25-1566) arose from IPR2023-01023 at the PTAB.
- Dismissal was by joint stipulation under Fed. R. App. P. 42(b), indicating the parties reached agreement—likely a settlement—before Federal Circuit merits review.
- Each side bears its own costs; no fee-shifting ordered.
- The mandate issued the same day as the order (May 1, 2026), making the dismissal immediately final.
- As a nonprecedential order, this has no binding effect on future cases.
